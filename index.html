Conference


ICEGZ’23 is the second edition of Saveetha Teaching Learning Centre (STLC), Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), India. This signature programme organized by STLC promises to connect the academic experts, budding educators and eminent members of the teaching-learning community. This confluence is all set to explore and enhance the potential of Engineering Educators and Learners. ICEGZ aims to provide a global platform to identify best practices in teaching and applaud the evolutionary aspects involved in reaching this zenith.

ICEGZ consists of two key note sessions and paper presentations under two tracks, on 5th May 2023. The research articles submitted to ICEGZ will be peer-reviewed and selected for prospective publications in SCOPUS INDEXED journals. ICEGZ’23 look forward to witnessing the works of passionate educators and learners to be a part of this event.

Guidelines for Submission

  • The authors are requested to submit the full-length manuscript with the page limits of 5-6 pages in the following link Click here
  • The paper must include an Abstract (not more than 150 words) and 3-5 Keywords.
  • Full paper must be in Times New Roman (normal style and font size 12) and in MS-Word. The page size should be A4, double column with 2.5 cm margin on both sides with single line spacing. All pages of the manuscript (including Tables and Figures) should be numbered.
  • After the intimation of selection, the participants need to prepare a Camera Ready Copy for final submission.

Journals


ICEGZ 23 publish research articles in prominent journals (SCOPUS INDEXED)

  • Journal of Engineering Education Transformations(JEET)

Publishing a Paper in the above-mentioned Journals requires Paper Processing Charges that have to be paid by the submitting author upon acceptance of the Paper for publication following the below criteria.


  • Peer review would be the first process in the selection of a paper which will be evaluated by a group of experts in the same field to make sure it meets the necessary standards for acceptance and publication.
  • Peer review for publication is completely based on the Journal's decision
  • Review occurs during a fixed window of time. All authors are notified of the decision on their paper at the same time after evaluation.
  • Reviewers will give a clear statement of recommodation for each paper and supply comments to support their recommendation suitable for transmission to the author.
  • Authors should agree to comply with the ethical policy of the agreed Journal and understand that publisher has the right to investigate any suspicions and/or allegations of misconduct relating to publications.
